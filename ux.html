User Experience (UX) Design

Designing websites that are easy to use, intuitive, and meaningful for users.

Image

Overview

User experience design focuses on creating websites that are easy to navigate, intuitive, and accessible for a wide range of users. Good UX design considers how users interact with a site and ensures that information is easy to find and understand.

Key Concepts

Recognition over Recall

Users should recognize options rather than remember them.

Consistency

Layouts and interactions should remain consistent across pages.

Visual Hierarchy

Important information should stand out clearly to guide users.

What I Learned

UX design seems to have a lot of sub-categories. When someone experiences anything, there's so much at play because we humans generally see, hear, feel, and do things all at the same time. Making sure things are optimized, accessible, secure, and responsive provides the conclusive user experience.
